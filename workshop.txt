description=[list]
description=[*]Craft and place a Shipping Terminal.
description=[*]Use the trash container terminal tile as the sender/receiver.
description=[*]Activate the terminal near a powered central.
description=[*]Drop items on the floor of the terminal tile.
description=[*]Use right-click and choose a registered destination.
description=[*]Items are transferred floor-to-floor between shipping terminals.
description=[*]Remote Multiplayer destinations now stay synced much more reliably, even with no players near the target area.
